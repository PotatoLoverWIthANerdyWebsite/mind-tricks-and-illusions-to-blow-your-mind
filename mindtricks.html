Mind Trick Number Three: Color, Color.

Try to say the color on the word, and not the word.

Now you may notice this is hard; that's because there is a conflict between the left side of you brain and the right side. The right side of your brain, the creative side, tries to read the color, but the left side of your brain, the "smart" side, insists on reading the word.
